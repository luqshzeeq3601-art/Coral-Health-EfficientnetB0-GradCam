About the Project

Automating Global Reef Monitoring

Coral Health AI is trained on the BHD Coral Dataset from Kaggle — a curated collection of 1,582 underwater coral images classified into three health states: Healthy, Bleached, and Dead. Using EfficientNetB0 as the backbone with a 5-seed SWA ensemble approach, the project reports a canonical 98.11% held-out test accuracy under the documented 224x224 benchmark protocol.

The training pipeline employs Stochastic Weight Averaging (SWA), cosine decay learning rate scheduling, label smoothing, Mixup augmentation, and deployment-side calibration support — delivering robust, explainable coral health classifications with Grad-CAM visual explanations.

  • Trained on BHD Coral Dataset (1,582 images, 3 classes)
  • 5-seed ensemble with SWA for robust predictions
  • Canonical 224x224 ensemble benchmark with separate protocol analysis
  • Grad-CAM explainability for every prediction
